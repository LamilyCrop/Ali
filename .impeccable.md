## Design Context

### Users
American Lighting Industry Corp serves commercial and industrial lighting buyers, distributors, contractors, OEM partners, and specifiers who need to evaluate fixtures, accessories, compliance, and support information quickly.

### Brand Personality
Restrained, credible, industrial. The interface should feel calm and precise rather than promotional, with emphasis on confidence, clarity, and operational reliability.

### Aesthetic Direction
Refined grayscale with subtle cool neutrals, minimal ornament, and product-forward imagery. Avoid loud gradients, saturated accents, and novelty effects. Favor clean surfaces, thin borders, generous spacing, and sober typography.

### Design Principles
- Reduce visual noise so product and compliance information carries the page.
- Use a single neutral palette with contrast coming from value, not hue.
- Prefer structure, spacing, and typography over decoration.
- Keep motion understated and functional.
- Make trust signals and navigation obvious without feeling heavy.

Inferred from the existing ALI marketing copy and the March 19, 2026 request to make the site more minimal and grayscale.
